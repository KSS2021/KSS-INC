About Our Business

Kamaljeet Singh & Son Inc is a wholesale and distribution business based in Colonia, New Jersey. We supply convenience stores, grocery stores, mini-marts, variety stores, and other retail businesses with quality everyday products at competitive wholesale prices.

Our goal is to help retailers find fast-moving products, reliable supply, and professional service. We focus on general merchandise, cleaning supplies, personal care items, household products, grocery items, and more.
